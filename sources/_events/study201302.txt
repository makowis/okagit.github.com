岡山Git勉強会 201302
====================

* 場所： `倉敷労働会館 <http://www.sanbg.com/roukan/>`_ 大会議室(207) （ `地図 <https://maps.google.co.jp/maps?f=q&source=embed&hl=ja&geocode=&q=%E5%80%89%E6%95%B7%E5%8A%B4%E5%83%8D%E4%BC%9A%E9%A4%A8&sll=35.46074,133.054562&sspn=0.032159,0.052614&brcurrent=3,0x3551564ee4d4d091:0x933c361d7363d337,0&ie=UTF8&hq=%E5%80%89%E6%95%B7%E5%8A%B4%E5%83%8D%E4%BC%9A%E9%A4%A8&hnear=&radius=15000&ll=34.615268,133.770046&spn=0.049446,0.077248&z=13&iwloc=A>`_ ）
